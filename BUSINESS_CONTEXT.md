## Cancellation & Refund Policy

APY operates on a **credit-first system**. Refunds are strongly discouraged — they are affecting cash flow significantly.

### Customer Cancellations
| Timing | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| More than 24 hours before class | Full class credit (no expiry) |
| Less than 24 hours before class | No credits or refunds (exceptions for documented emergencies at APY's discretion) |
| No-shows | No credits or refunds |

### APY-Initiated Cancellations
- Guests automatically receive a full class credit
- Guests may request a refund **within 48 hours** of cancellation notice only
- After 48 hours: credits are the only option
- Approved refunds processed within **7-14 business days**

### Rebooking
- Email afropuppyyogaofficial@gmail.com with proof of original ticket
- APY issues a unique rebooking code (100% value, one-time use, no expiry)

### Ticket Transfers
- Tickets are transferable to another person
- Must notify APY before class start time

### Late Arrivals
- Arrive 10-15 minutes early
- Entry may be denied once class has started
- No refunds or credits for late arrivals
